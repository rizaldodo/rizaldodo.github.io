About

First of all I’d like to introduce about my self, my name is Muhammad Syahrizal Widodo I’m 23 years old, I’m graduated from Game Technology PENS(Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya) in 2018. I have been an Iphone users for years and admirer of the sophistication of the design and performance of Iphone apps. The user interface (UI) is always aesthetically pleasing and intuitively engaging, hence giving me a distinguished user experience. This experience has sparked great interest to me to be an Apple developer one day. It landed me at the Game Technology Department to study the core of game development. When in college I meet iOS community but the community is not bright enough I thought.

I believe that to become a distinguished, not only successful, game developer hard skill alone is not enough. Choosing the right development platform is also important. A good development platform shall be able to harmoniously optimize the hardware and software (including middleware and firmware) to give maximum user experience. Apple devices (PC, Apple TV, tablets and phones) are manufactured solely by Apple Inc. which enable strict control of device specification to adhere to certain standards. This gives guarantee of device performance which is an important element to produce consistent user experience across devices.
